# Dancing Brush Website Requirements

> Source: Website request email received on July 14, 2026
>
> This document organizes the initial ideas and requirements for the Dancing Brush publishing website. Items marked **To discuss** are not final decisions.

## 1. Project Goal

Create an official bilingual website for Dancing Brush that introduces the publisher, its books, lectures, downloadable resources, news, and purchasing links.

The website should feel trustworthy, professional, and suitable for both Korean and international visitors.

## 2. Reference Websites

- [글로연 출판사](http://gloyeon.com/)
  - Korean and English language selection on the first page
- [아스터로이드북](https://asteroidbook.com/)
- [북극곰 출판사](https://bookgoodcome.com/)
  - Multi-language translation options near the bottom of the website
- [Penguin Books UK](https://www.penguin.co.uk/)
  - Cookie preference/settings interface

These websites are references for structure, visual direction, multilingual navigation, and publishing-related content presentation. The final site does not need to copy their layouts.

## 3. Language Strategy

### Primary languages

- Korean
- English

### Preferred approach

Provide clear Korean and English entry points either:

1. On the first landing screen, similar to the 글로연 website, or
2. Through a prominent language selector in the main header.

### Additional languages

Automatic translation into other languages may reduce trust when translations are inaccurate.

Recommended structure:

- Maintain manually prepared Korean and English pages as the official content.
- Optionally provide automatic translation for other languages as a secondary convenience feature.
- Clearly distinguish automatically translated content from official Korean and English content.

**To discuss:** Whether additional automatic translation is necessary for the first release.

## 4. Cookie and Privacy Requirements

The Penguin website presents visitors with cookie settings.

Whether Dancing Brush needs a cookie consent interface depends on the final features used by the site, such as:

- Visitor analytics
- Advertising or tracking tools
- Embedded third-party media
- Social media widgets
- Visitors from regions with cookie consent requirements

**To discuss:** Decide after the hosting, analytics, embedded services, and target audience are finalized. A simple static website without non-essential tracking may require only a minimal privacy notice rather than a complex cookie settings panel.

## 5. Official Domain Email

Preferred official email format:

- `name@choomboot.com`

A domain-based email account should improve the professional appearance and trustworthiness of the publisher.

Possible implementation options include:

- Google Workspace
- Another email hosting service connected to `choomboot.com`
- Email forwarding from the domain to an existing Gmail inbox

**To discuss:** Confirm domain ownership, DNS access, required email addresses, number of users, and whether full mailbox hosting or simple forwarding is preferred.

## 8. Recommended Navigation Simplification

The initial menu contains many items. To keep the first release understandable, the top-level navigation could be simplified to:

### Korean

- 홈
- 춤붓 소개
- 책
- 강의
- 자료실
- 새소식
- 구매하기

Software and artworks could initially appear as sections under `새소식`, `구매하기`, or a compact `더보기` menu until enough content exists to justify separate top-level menus.

### English

- Home
- About
- Books
- Lectures
- Downloads
- News
- Buy

Software and artworks can be added later as standalone menus when their content, branding, and sales flow are ready.
